SM Mining Africa pays referral commissions across four levels of your network. This page explains the commission rate at each level, how commissions are calculated, and how and when payouts are processed.

## Commission Structure

Each level of your referral network earns you a different commission rate. Deeper levels carry higher rates, rewarding you for cultivating a wide and active network.

| Level | Relationship | Commission Rate |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| L1 | Direct referrals — people you personally invite | 5% |
| L2 | Referrals made by your L1 network | 10% |
| L3 | Referrals made by your L2 network | 15% |
| L4 | Referrals made by your L3 network | 20% |

## How Commissions Are Calculated

Your commission is calculated as a percentage of the **investment amount** made by each referral when they activate a plan. The percentage applied depends on that investor's level within your network.

**Example:** If your L1 referral invests $1,000, you earn $50 (5% of $1,000). If their referral — your L2 — invests $2,000, you earn $200 (10% of $2,000).

### Worked Example

The table below shows how commissions stack up across all four levels from a single active network:

| Referral Level | Investment Amount | Your Rate | Your Earnings |
|----------------|-------------------|-----------|---------------|
| L1 — Direct referral | $1,000 | 5% | $50 |
| L2 — Referral of your L1 | $2,000 | 10% | $200 |
| L3 — Referral of your L2 | $500 | 15% | $75 |
| L4 — Referral of your L3 | $5,000 | 20% | $1,000 |
| **Total earned** | | | **$1,325** |

<Tip>
  Building a strong L2 and L3 network is where the biggest earning potential lies — your L4 rate of 20% can generate significant passive income. Focus on helping your direct referrals grow their own networks to unlock the highest commission tiers.
</Tip>

## When Are Commissions Paid?

Commissions are credited **automatically** to your wallet balance the moment a referred investor activates a plan. There is no waiting period, no manual approval process, and no claim required on your part. As soon as your referral's plan is active, your commission appears in your wallet.

<Note>
  Commission rates apply to the initial investment amount of each plan activation, not compounded on renewals unless the referral activates a new plan. Each fresh plan activation by a referral triggers a new commission credit at the applicable level rate.
</Note>

## Withdrawing Commission Earnings

All referral commissions are added directly to your available wallet balance — the same balance used for investments and other platform earnings. You can:

- **Withdraw** your commission earnings to your preferred payment method at any time, following the standard withdrawal process.
- **Reinvest** your commissions by using your wallet balance to activate a new mining or trading plan, compounding your overall returns.

There is no separate commission wallet. Your referral earnings are immediately spendable and withdrawable alongside any other funds in your account.
