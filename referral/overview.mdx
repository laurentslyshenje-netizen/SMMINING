The SM Mining Africa referral program lets you earn passive income by introducing new investors to the platform. With a four-level commission structure, your earnings grow as your referral network expands — the more active investors you bring in, the more you earn from every level of your network.

## How the Referral Program Works

SM Mining Africa uses a multi-level referral system that rewards you not just for the investors you directly invite, but also for the networks those investors build themselves.

- **Your referral link** — You receive a unique referral link from your dashboard that tracks every signup and investment made through it.
- **Level 1 referrals** — When someone registers and invests using your link, they become your Level 1 referral. You earn a commission on every plan they activate.
- **Level 2 referrals** — When your Level 1 referral shares their own link and brings in a new investor, that investor becomes your Level 2 referral. You earn commissions from their activity too.
- **Level 3 and Level 4 referrals** — The chain continues. Investors brought in by your Level 2 network become your Level 3 referrals, and investors brought in by your Level 3 network become your Level 4 referrals — all generating commissions for you automatically.

## Getting Your Referral Link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your dashboard">
    Sign in to your SM Mining Africa account at the main platform login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Referrals">
    Select **Referrals** from the navigation menu to open your referral hub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your unique referral link">
    Click **Copy Link** to copy your personalised referral URL to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share your link">
    Distribute your link via social media, email, WhatsApp, Telegram, or any other messaging platform to start building your network.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Share your referral link in investment communities, social media groups, and with friends who are interested in cryptocurrency mining. Targeted sharing in active investment spaces yields the best results.
</Tip>

## Tracking Your Referrals

Your referral dashboard gives you a complete, real-time view of your entire network and earnings. From the **Referrals** section of your account, you can monitor:

- **Referrals per level** — See how many active investors you have at each of your four referral levels (L1 through L4).
- **Total commissions earned** — View the cumulative commissions credited to your account across all levels and all time.
- **Pending commissions** — Track any commissions that are queued and awaiting confirmation from recently activated plans.
- **Payout history** — Review a full log of every commission payment credited to your wallet, including the date, level, and amount for each entry.

<Note>
  Commissions are credited to your wallet automatically when your referrals complete investments. You do not need to request or manually claim your referral earnings.
</Note>
