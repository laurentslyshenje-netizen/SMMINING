SM Mining Africa integrates with MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5, the industry-standard trading platforms trusted by millions of traders worldwide. Once connected, you can receive signal alerts directly inside MetaTrader and execute trades from the same platform — no switching between apps required.

<Tip>
  Install MetaTrader on your mobile device so you can act on signals instantly, wherever you are.
</Tip>

## Why Use MetaTrader

Connecting MetaTrader to your SM Mining Africa account gives you access to a powerful suite of professional trading tools:

- **Advanced charting** — analyse price action with dozens of built-in technical indicators and drawing tools
- **One-click order execution** — open and close trades instantly at the levels specified in your signal
- **Expert Advisors (EAs)** — automate trade execution with algorithmic strategies running around the clock
- **Mobile and desktop apps** — trade from your phone, tablet, or computer with a consistent interface
- **Full trading history** — review every trade you have placed, including entry and exit prices and profit/loss

## Getting MetaTrader

Download MetaTrader directly from MetaQuotes or through your broker's branded version:

- **MetaTrader 4:** [https://www.metatrader4.com/en/download](https://www.metatrader4.com/en/download)
- **MetaTrader 5:** your broker's website will provide a direct MT5 download link

Install the application on your preferred device and log in using the credentials provided by your forex broker. If you do not yet have a live or demo broker account, open one before proceeding to the linking step below.

## Linking Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SM Mining Africa">
    Open your SM Mining Africa dashboard and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to MetaTrader integration settings">
    Go to **Settings > Integrations > MetaTrader**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your MetaTrader credentials">
    Fill in the following fields:
    - **Broker server name** — found in your broker's welcome email or the MT4/MT5 login screen
    - **MT4/MT5 login ID** — your numeric account number assigned by your broker
    - **Investor password** — the read-only password for your trading account (not your master password)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your account">
    Click **Connect**. SM Mining Africa verifies the credentials against your broker's server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    A confirmation message appears once the link is established. From this point forward, signals published by SM Mining Africa analysts are pushed directly to your MetaTrader terminal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Always use your **investor (read-only) password** when connecting MetaTrader to SM Mining Africa — never enter your master trading password. The investor password lets the platform deliver signal alerts without the ability to place or modify trades on your behalf.
</Note>

## Executing Signals in MetaTrader

When a signal arrives in your MetaTrader terminal:

1. Open the **Market Watch** panel and locate the currency pair listed in the signal.
2. Right-click the pair and select **New Order** (MT4) or **New Trade** (MT5).
3. Set the trade direction to **Buy** or **Sell** as specified.
4. Enter the **entry price** from the signal. If the market is already at that level, use a market order; if not, place a limit or stop order.
5. Set the **Stop Loss** and **Take Profit** fields to the exact levels provided in the signal.
6. Confirm your lot size based on your risk management rules, then click **Place**.

Review the trade in the **Terminal** window to confirm it is live and that your stop-loss and take-profit levels are attached correctly.

## Using Expert Advisors

Advanced users can automate signal execution with Expert Advisors. An EA running on your MetaTrader terminal can monitor incoming alerts and open or close trades automatically based on pre-defined rules — removing the need to be at your screen when a signal arrives.

To get started with EAs, open the **MetaEditor** inside MetaTrader, write or import your automation script, and attach it to the relevant currency pair chart. Ensure **AutoTrading** is enabled in the MetaTrader toolbar before activating any EA.
