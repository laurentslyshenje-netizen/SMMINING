SM Mining Africa's forex signals service delivers curated trade alerts directly to your email and phone. Each signal includes the currency pair, entry price, stop-loss, and take-profit targets — everything you need to act quickly and confidently in the market.

<Tip>
  Enable both email and SMS delivery so you never miss a time-sensitive signal.
</Tip>

## What's Included in a Signal

Every signal our analysts publish contains the following components:

- **Currency pair** (e.g., EUR/USD, GBP/JPY)
- **Direction** — Buy or Sell
- **Entry price** — the recommended level at which to open the trade
- **Stop-loss level** — the price at which to close the trade to limit losses
- **Take-profit target(s)** — one or more price levels to lock in gains
- **Analyst commentary** — context explaining the market reasoning behind the call

Here is an example of what a published signal looks like:

```
Signal: EUR/USD BUY
Entry: 1.0850
Stop Loss: 1.0790
Take Profit 1: 1.0920
Take Profit 2: 1.0980
Analyst Note: Strong bullish momentum following ECB rate decision.
```

## How to Subscribe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Signals section">
    Log in to your SM Mining Africa account and navigate to **Dashboard > Signals**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your delivery method">
    Select your preferred notification channel — **Email**, **SMS**, or **Both**. We recommend enabling both to maximise your chances of catching every signal in time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your subscription">
    Review the confirmation prompt and click **Subscribe**. Your preferences are saved immediately and signals will begin arriving via your chosen channel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Acting on Signals

When you receive a signal, open your broker trading platform and locate the currency pair specified in the alert. Enter the trade in the direction indicated (Buy or Sell) using the exact entry price, stop-loss, and take-profit levels provided.

Place your order type according to whether the market is currently at the entry price (a market order) or has not yet reached it (a limit or stop order). Always apply the stop-loss and at least the first take-profit target before confirming the trade.

For step-by-step instructions on placing trades inside MetaTrader 4 or MetaTrader 5, see the [MetaTrader setup guide](/trading/metatrader).

## Signal Performance

Your SM Mining Africa dashboard keeps a full record of every signal issued, including outcome and success rate over time. Navigate to **Dashboard > Signals > History** to review past performance, filter by currency pair or date range, and track how each call played out.

<Note>
  Signals are provided for informational purposes only. Always manage your risk carefully and use appropriate position sizing relative to your account balance. Past signal performance does not guarantee future results.
</Note>
