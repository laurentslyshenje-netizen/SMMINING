This guide walks you through everything you need to get up and running on SM Mining Africa — from creating your account and funding your wallet to activating your first investment plan and tracking your returns. Follow the steps below and you'll be earning in under 10 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Your Account">
    Navigate to [smminingafrica.com/register](https://smminingafrica.com/register) and fill in your details:

    - **Full Name** — enter your first and last name as they appear on your ID
    - **Email Address** — use an active email address you can access immediately
    - **Password** — choose a strong, unique password

    After submitting the form, check your inbox for a verification email from SM Mining Africa and click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In and Complete Your Profile">
    Once your email is verified, log in to your dashboard at [smminingafrica.com/login](https://smminingafrica.com/login).

    Before you invest, take a moment to secure and complete your account:

    - Go to **Settings → Security** and enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** using an authenticator app or SMS
    - Navigate to **Settings → Profile** and fill in your phone number, country, and upload a profile photo

    Completing your profile ensures smooth withdrawals and access to all platform features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deposit Funds">
    To fund your wallet, follow these steps:

    1. Go to **Wallet → Deposit** from your dashboard
    2. Select your preferred cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum)
    3. Copy the wallet address shown on screen — or scan the QR code
    4. Send your desired amount from your external wallet or exchange to that address

    <Note>
      Deposits are credited to your SM Mining Africa wallet within a few minutes after the required number of blockchain confirmations is reached. Confirmation times vary by network.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate an Investment Plan">
    With funds in your wallet, you're ready to start earning:

    1. Navigate to **Investment Plans** from the main menu
    2. Browse the available packages and select the one that matches your goals and budget
    3. Enter the amount you wish to invest
    4. Review the expected returns and plan duration, then click **Confirm**

    Your plan activates immediately, and returns begin accruing according to the plan schedule.

    <Tip>
      If you're new to crypto mining investments, start with the **Starter Plan**. It requires a lower minimum deposit and lets you get familiar with how returns and withdrawals work before scaling up.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Your Returns">
    Head back to your **Dashboard** at any time to monitor your portfolio:

    - **Active Plans** — view all currently running investment plans
    - **Expected Returns** — see projected earnings for each plan
    - **Wallet Balance** — track your total available and locked balance
    - **Transaction History** — review all deposits, withdrawals, and plan activations

    Returns are credited to your wallet automatically on schedule. Once available, you can reinvest or withdraw at any time from **Wallet → Withdraw**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
