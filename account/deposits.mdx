SM Mining Africa accepts deposits in major cryptocurrencies. Once your funds arrive on the blockchain, they are credited to your wallet and you can immediately activate an investment plan.

## Supported Deposit Methods

SM Mining Africa supports the following cryptocurrencies for deposits:

- **BTC** – Bitcoin
- **ETH** – Ethereum
- **USDT** – Tether
- **BNB** – BNB
- **DOGE** – Dogecoin
- **LTC** – Litecoin
- **DASH** – Dash
- **ETC** – Ethereum Classic
- **BCH** – Bitcoin Cash

All deposited assets are converted to their **USD equivalent** and reflected in your account balance accordingly.

## How to Make a Deposit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your dashboard">
    Visit the SM Mining Africa website and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Wallet > Deposit">
    Click **Wallet** in the main navigation, then select **Deposit** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your cryptocurrency">
    Select the cryptocurrency you want to send from the list of supported assets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your wallet address">
    Copy the wallet address displayed on the screen, or scan the QR code using your mobile wallet app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send funds from your external wallet">
    Open your external wallet or exchange account and send the desired amount to the address you copied.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for blockchain confirmation">
    Allow time for the network to confirm your transaction. Confirmation times typically range from **1 to 30 minutes** depending on the cryptocurrency and network congestion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your updated balance">
    Your SM Mining Africa balance updates automatically once the required number of confirmations is reached. No manual action is needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Always copy your wallet address directly from your dashboard — never type it manually. Sending funds to an incorrect address results in **permanent, unrecoverable loss** of those funds.
</Warning>

<Note>
  Each deposit generates a new wallet address. Always return to your dashboard to retrieve the current address before making a new deposit. Do not reuse addresses from previous transactions.
</Note>

## Minimum Deposit Amounts

Minimum deposit amounts vary by asset. The table below shows common minimums:

| Asset | Minimum Deposit |
|-------|----------------|
| BTC   | 0.0005 BTC     |
| ETH   | 0.01 ETH       |
| USDT  | $10 equivalent |

For current minimums on all supported assets, visit the **Wallet** page in your dashboard, where up-to-date limits are always displayed.

## Deposit Confirmations

For security, SM Mining Africa requires a minimum number of blockchain confirmations before crediting deposits to your balance:

| Network  | Required Confirmations |
|----------|------------------------|
| Bitcoin (BTC)  | 3 confirmations  |
| Ethereum (ETH) | 12 confirmations |
| Others         | Varies by network — check your Wallet page for details |

Confirmation times depend on network traffic and the transaction fee applied by your sending wallet. Using a higher fee generally speeds up confirmation.
