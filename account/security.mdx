Your account security is your first line of defense for protecting your investments. SM Mining Africa provides multiple security features — enabling them takes only a few minutes and significantly reduces your risk.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of verification when you log in, making it much harder for unauthorized users to access your account even if they obtain your password. **Enabling 2FA is strongly recommended for all accounts.**

### How to Enable 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Sign in to your SM Mining Africa dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Security">
    Click your profile icon or **Settings** in the navigation, then select the **Security** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Enable 2FA">
    Locate the Two-Factor Authentication section and click **Enable 2FA**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your 2FA method">
    Select either **Authenticator App** (recommended) or **SMS** as your preferred verification method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the setup">
    Follow the on-screen instructions to link your authenticator app or verify your phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your backup codes">
    Store the backup codes provided in a safe, offline location. You will need these if you ever lose access to your 2FA device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Recommended authenticator apps:**

- [Google Authenticator](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/1066447)
- [Authy](https://authy.com/)
- [Microsoft Authenticator](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/mobile-authenticator-app)

<Warning>
  Never share your password, 2FA codes, or account recovery codes with anyone — including SM Mining Africa support staff. Legitimate support will never ask for this information.
</Warning>

## Strong Password Practices

A strong, unique password is your account's primary barrier against unauthorized access. Follow these guidelines to keep your password secure:

- Use **at least 12 characters** combining uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
- **Never reuse passwords** across different platforms or services.
- **Change your password immediately** if you suspect it has been compromised or exposed in a data breach.

<Tip>
  Use a password manager such as Bitwarden, 1Password, or LastPass to generate and securely store strong, unique passwords for every platform you use.
</Tip>

## Recognizing Phishing Attempts

Phishing attacks trick you into handing over your credentials by impersonating trusted services. Protect yourself by staying alert:

- **SM Mining Africa will never ask for your password via email**, direct message, or any other channel.
- Always access the platform by navigating directly to the **official SM Mining Africa URL** — do not click links in unsolicited emails or messages.
- Before entering your login credentials, verify that the URL in your browser's address bar is correct and shows a valid HTTPS connection (padlock icon).
- If you receive a suspicious email claiming to be from SM Mining Africa, do not click any links — report it to support immediately.

## Login Activity Monitoring

Regularly reviewing your login history helps you spot unauthorized access early. To check your activity:

1. Go to **Settings > Security > Login Activity** in your dashboard.
2. Review the list of recent logins, including timestamps and locations.
3. If you see any login you do not recognize, **change your password immediately** and contact SM Mining Africa support.

## What to Do If Your Account Is Compromised

If you believe your account has been accessed without your authorization, act immediately:

1. **Change your password** — Go to Settings > Security and update your password right away.
2. **Revoke all active sessions** — In Settings > Security, end all active sessions to sign out any unauthorized devices.
3. **Contact support** — Report the incident through the [SM Mining Africa support page](/support/contact) so the team can assist you and investigate.

Taking these steps quickly minimizes the risk of financial loss and helps secure your account.
