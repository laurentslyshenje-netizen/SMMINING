Once your investment plan completes, your principal and returns are added to your available balance. You can withdraw these funds to any external cryptocurrency wallet at any time.

## Before You Withdraw

Make sure you meet the following conditions before submitting a withdrawal request:

- **Plan completion** — Confirm your investment plan cycle has ended and your returns have been credited to your available balance.
- **Verified contact details** — Ensure both your email address and phone number are confirmed on your account.
- **Identity verification (KYC)** — For larger withdrawal amounts, complete identity verification if prompted. This helps protect your account and comply with platform security requirements.

## How to Withdraw

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Wallet > Withdraw">
    Log in to your dashboard and click **Wallet** in the navigation, then select **Withdraw**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your cryptocurrency">
    Choose the cryptocurrency you want to receive in your external wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your external wallet address">
    Paste the destination wallet address from your personal wallet or exchange account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the withdrawal amount">
    Type the amount you wish to withdraw. Make sure it meets the minimum withdrawal requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the transaction summary">
    Check the summary carefully — confirm the recipient address, withdrawal amount, network fee, and estimated amount you will receive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with your password or 2FA code">
    Enter your account password or your two-factor authentication (2FA) code to authorize the withdrawal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Click **Confirm** to submit your withdrawal request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email confirmation">
    You will receive an email confirming your withdrawal request. Funds are sent to your wallet within **1–24 hours**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Double-check your destination wallet address before confirming. Cryptocurrency withdrawals are **irreversible once sent** — SM Mining Africa cannot recover funds sent to an incorrect address.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  If you plan to reinvest, you can activate a new investment plan directly from your available balance — no need to withdraw and re-deposit first.
</Tip>

## Withdrawal Minimums & Fees

| Detail              | Value                                                         |
|---------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Minimum withdrawal  | $20 USD equivalent                                            |
| Network fee         | Varies by cryptocurrency and current network conditions       |
| Processing time     | 1–24 hours (most withdrawals are processed within a few hours)|

Network fees are deducted from the withdrawal amount. The exact fee is displayed in the transaction summary before you confirm.

## Withdrawal Status

Track your withdrawal at any time by going to **Wallet > History** in your dashboard. Each withdrawal entry displays one of the following statuses:

| Status       | Meaning                                                              |
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Pending**  | Your request has been received and is awaiting processing.           |
| **Processing** | The transaction is being prepared and broadcast to the blockchain. |
| **Completed**  | The funds have been sent to your external wallet address.          |

If your withdrawal remains in **Pending** status for more than 24 hours, contact SM Mining Africa support for assistance.
