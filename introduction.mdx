## How the Platform Works

**Mining Investment:** You select a plan that matches your capital and return goals, make your deposit, and the platform immediately allocates your funds to a professionally managed mining pool. You do not need to manage hardware, software, or infrastructure — SM Mining Africa handles all operational aspects on your behalf. Your returns are calculated and credited to your account automatically at the end of each 7-day cycle.

**Trading Signals:** A team of professional analysts monitors forex markets around the clock, seven days a week. When a high-probability setup is identified, you receive a signal containing a precise entry price, a stop-loss level to cap your downside, and a take-profit target. You execute the trade through your own brokerage account, keeping full control of your capital at every step.

**Referral Network:** Sharing your unique referral link is all it takes to start earning commissions. When someone you refer makes an investment — and when their referrals invest, and so on — you earn automatically, up to four levels deep. Commission rates increase with each level, ranging from 5% at level one up to 20% at level four, rewarding you more as your network grows.

## Capital Access Program

This program is designed for experienced traders who have demonstrated consistent performance and want to scale beyond what their own funds allow.
