Taking a few minutes to fully set up your account before you invest protects your funds, ensures you receive important alerts, and prevents delays when you're ready to withdraw. This guide covers everything from registration and email verification to securing your account with 2FA and configuring your notification preferences.

---

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Registration Page">
    Open your browser and navigate to [smminingafrica.com/register](https://smminingafrica.com/register).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill In Your Details">
    Complete the registration form with the following information:

    - **Full Name** — enter your legal first and last name
    - **Email Address** — use a valid, active email you check regularly
    - **Password** — create a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

    Review the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then check the agreement box.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit and Check Your Inbox">
    Click **Create Account**. SM Mining Africa will send a verification email to the address you provided. Open that email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Verify Your Email

After registering, you'll receive an email from SM Mining Africa with the subject line **"Verify Your Email Address"**. Here's what to do:

1. Open the email in your inbox (check your **Spam** or **Promotions** folder if you don't see it within a couple of minutes)
2. Click the **Verify Email** button or copy-paste the verification link into your browser
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard, confirming your email is active

If the link has expired or the email never arrived, log in to your account and go to **Settings → Profile** to request a new verification email.

---

## Complete Your Profile

A complete profile is required for full platform access, including withdrawals. Navigate to **Settings → Profile** and provide the following:

- **Full Name** — confirm or update your legal name
- **Phone Number** — enter a mobile number you can receive SMS messages on
- **Country** — select your country of residence from the dropdown
- **Profile Photo** — upload a clear photo (JPEG or PNG, under 5 MB)

Save your changes once all fields are filled in. A complete profile also speeds up any identity verification steps required for larger withdrawals.

<Warning>
  Withdrawals above certain thresholds may require you to complete **Know Your Customer (KYC) identity verification**. This typically involves submitting a government-issued ID and a selfie. Complete this step early to avoid delays when you're ready to withdraw your earnings.
</Warning>

## Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds a critical second layer of security to your account. Even if your password is compromised, 2FA prevents unauthorised access.

To enable 2FA:

1. Go to **Settings → Security** from your dashboard
2. Under **Two-Factor Authentication**, click **Enable**
3. Choose your preferred method:
   - **Authenticator App** (recommended) — scan the QR code using Google Authenticator, Authy, or any TOTP-compatible app, then enter the 6-digit code to confirm
   - **SMS Authentication** — enter your phone number and verify the code sent to you
4. Save your backup codes in a secure location — you'll need them if you ever lose access to your 2FA device

Once enabled, you'll be prompted for a 2FA code every time you log in or initiate a withdrawal.

## Set Notification Preferences

Stay informed about your investments, trading signals, and account activity by configuring your notification settings.

Navigate to **Settings → Notifications** and configure the following alert types:

- **Investment Plan Updates** — get notified when a plan activates, pays out, or expires
- **Forex Trading Signals** — receive real-time alerts for new signals published by SM Mining Africa analysts
- **Deposit Confirmations** — be notified as soon as a deposit is credited to your wallet
- **Withdrawal Status** — receive updates at each stage of your withdrawal request
- **Security Alerts** — get notified of new logins, password changes, and 2FA updates

For each alert type, you can independently enable or disable:

- ✉️ **Email notifications**
- 📱 **SMS notifications**

<Tip>
  Enable **both email and SMS notifications** for forex trading signals. Signals are time-sensitive — having two alert channels ensures you never miss an opportunity, even if one notification is delayed.
</Tip>

Save your preferences once you're done. You can return to this page at any time to update your settings.
