## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is SM Mining Africa?">
    SM Mining Africa is a cryptocurrency mining investment and forex trading signals platform. You invest in mining plans, receive real-time trading signals, and earn returns managed by a team of professional analysts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
    Visit [smminingafrica.com/register](https://smminingafrica.com/register), fill in your name, email, and password, then verify your email. Your account is active instantly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is SM Mining Africa available in my country?">
    SM Mining Africa serves investors across Africa and internationally. Check the platform during registration for any regional restrictions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deposits & Withdrawals

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What cryptocurrencies can I deposit?">
    We support BTC, ETH, USDT, BNB, DOGE, LTC, DASH, ETC, and BCH.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does a deposit take?">
    Deposits are credited after blockchain confirmation — typically 1–30 minutes depending on network congestion and the required number of confirmations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the minimum deposit?">
    Minimums vary by asset. The overall minimum is equivalent to $10 for USDT. Check **Wallet > Deposit** in your dashboard for asset-specific minimums.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When can I withdraw my funds?">
    You can withdraw your available balance at any time. For plan earnings, you must wait until the 7-day plan cycle completes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long do withdrawals take?">
    Most withdrawals are processed within 1–24 hours. You will receive an email notification when funds are sent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Investment Plans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does each investment plan run?">
    All plans have a 7-day duration. Your principal and returns are credited to your wallet when the plan expires.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel a plan before it expires?">
    Plans cannot be cancelled early once activated. Your funds are committed for the full 7-day cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run multiple plans at once?">
    Yes. You can activate multiple plans simultaneously to diversify your investments across different tiers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are returns guaranteed?">
    Returns are estimated based on historical mining performance and are not guaranteed. Mining profitability can vary with market conditions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Referrals & Signals

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the referral program work?">
    You earn commissions when people you refer invest on the platform. The commission structure goes 4 levels deep — 5%, 10%, 15%, and 20% respectively.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When are referral commissions credited?">
    Commissions are credited automatically to your wallet when a referred investor activates a plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I receive forex signals?">
    Subscribe to signals from your dashboard under **Signals**. Choose email, SMS, or both as your delivery method.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use the signals with any broker?">
    Yes. Signals include all the information you need (pair, direction, entry, stop-loss, take-profit) to place trades with any broker or via MetaTrader.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    Click **Forgot Password** on the login page. Enter your registered email and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I enable two-factor authentication?">
    Go to **Settings > Security > Enable 2FA**. Choose your preferred method (authenticator app or SMS) and follow the setup steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What do I do if I suspect unauthorized account access?">
    Change your password immediately, revoke all sessions from **Settings > Security**, and contact support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
