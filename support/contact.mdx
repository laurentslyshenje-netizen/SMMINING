The SM Mining Africa support team is available to help you with any questions about your account, investments, or platform features. Use the options below to get in touch.

## Live Chat

Access live chat support 24/7 directly on the platform. Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of any page after logging in to start an instant conversation with a support agent.

## Email Support

Send a detailed message to **support@smminingafrica.com** and a member of the team will follow up with you.

When emailing, make sure to:

- Include your registered email address
- Provide a clear description of the issue
- Attach any relevant screenshots or documentation

**Response time:** Within 24 hours on business days.

## What to Include in Your Request

To help the support team resolve your issue as quickly as possible, include the following in every request:

- Your registered email address
- A clear description of your issue
- Any relevant transaction IDs or plan numbers
- Screenshots if applicable

## Response Times

| Channel   | Availability | Response Time    |
|-----------|--------------|------------------|
| Live Chat | 24/7         | Immediate        |
| Email     | 24/7         | Within 24 hours  |

<Tip>
  For the fastest resolution, include your transaction ID or plan reference number in any support request.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  SM Mining Africa support will **never** ask for your password, 2FA codes, or private keys. Requests for this information are fraudulent.
</Warning>
