SM Mining Africa offers five investment packages, each designed for a different level of capital commitment and return expectation. All packages run on the same 7-day cycle, so you always know exactly when your returns will be credited. Use the comparison below to identify the package that fits your investment goals, then dive into the details to understand exactly what each tier delivers.

<Tip>
  Start with the Starter plan to experience the full 7-day cycle and see how the platform works before committing larger capital.
</Tip>

## Package Comparison

| Package  | Duration | Min Investment | Target Return | Best For                                               |
|----------|----------|----------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| Starter  | 7 days   | $50            | 5–8%          | First-time investors exploring the platform            |
| Silver   | 7 days   | $500           | 8–12%         | Investors ready to scale with modest capital           |
| Gold     | 7 days   | $2,000         | 12–18%        | Experienced investors seeking stronger yields          |
| Diamond  | 7 days   | $5,000         | 18–25%        | High-conviction investors with larger portfolios       |
| Platinum | 7 days   | $10,000        | 25–40%        | Institutional-grade capital and maximum returns        |

<Warning>
  Investment returns are estimated based on historical mining performance. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always invest only what you are prepared to commit for the full 7-day cycle.
</Warning>

## Package Details

**Starter**

The Starter package is the entry point into the SM Mining Africa ecosystem. With a minimum investment of just **$50**, it lets you activate your first plan with minimal risk and walk through the complete cycle — from activation to payout — so you can build confidence before scaling up. Target returns of **5–8%** over 7 days give you a meaningful result on a modest commitment.

---

**Silver**

The Silver package is built for investors who have taken their first steps and are ready to grow. Starting at **$500**, it unlocks a higher return range of **8–12%** over the same 7-day cycle. This tier suits investors who want to increase exposure while keeping their capital at a manageable level.

---

**Gold**

The Gold package is designed for experienced investors who are comfortable with the platform and want to pursue meaningfully stronger yields. The minimum commitment of **$2,000** positions your capital in a higher-performance segment of the mining pool, targeting returns of **12–18%** per 7-day cycle.

---

**Diamond**

The Diamond package is for high-conviction investors managing substantial portfolios. Starting at **$5,000**, this tier provides access to premium pool allocations and targets returns of **18–25%** over 7 days. It is the right choice if you are actively compounding capital across multiple cycles.

---

**Platinum**

The Platinum package represents the institutional-grade tier on the platform. With a minimum investment of **$10,000**, it provides maximum pool priority and the highest target return range of **25–40%** per 7-day cycle. Platinum is built for investors deploying serious capital and expecting top-tier performance.
