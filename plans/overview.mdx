SM Mining Africa offers tiered mining investment plans with 7-day durations. Each plan lets you allocate capital to a professionally managed mining pool and receive returns at the end of each cycle. Whether you are just getting started or managing a large portfolio, there is a plan designed to match your goals and risk appetite.

## How Plans Work

Every investment plan on SM Mining Africa operates on a fixed **7-day cycle**. Here is what happens from the moment you deposit to the moment your returns are credited:

1. **You deposit funds** into your SM Mining Africa wallet.
2. **You activate a plan** by selecting a package and committing your capital.
3. **The platform works on your behalf** — your capital is allocated to a professionally managed cryptocurrency mining pool and supported by forex trading signals, generating returns throughout the cycle.
4. **At the end of 7 days**, your principal and profits are credited to your wallet balance.
5. **You choose what to do next** — reinvest in a new cycle to compound your growth, or withdraw your earnings to your crypto wallet.

<Note>
  Plans automatically expire after 7 days. You must manually reinvest if you want to continue earning returns in a new cycle.
</Note>

## Choosing the Right Plan

Selecting the right package depends on two factors: **how much capital you want to commit** and **your investment experience**.

- **Starter ($50 minimum)** — Ideal if you are new to the platform and want to experience the cycle with minimal risk before scaling up.
- **Silver ($500 minimum)** — A natural next step once you are comfortable with how plans work and ready to grow your returns.
- **Gold ($2,000 minimum)** — Suited to experienced investors who want meaningfully stronger yields on a mid-range commitment.
- **Diamond ($5,000 minimum)** — Designed for high-conviction investors managing larger portfolios who want premium return potential.
- **Platinum ($10,000 minimum)** — The institutional-grade tier, built for maximum returns on significant capital.

<Tip>
  You can run multiple plans simultaneously to diversify your investment across different tiers and spread your risk.
</Tip>

## Activating a Plan

Follow these steps to activate an investment plan after funding your wallet:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Sign in at the SM Mining Africa platform using your registered email address and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Investment Plans">
    From your dashboard, select **Investment Plans** in the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a package">
    Browse the available packages — Starter, Silver, Gold, Diamond, or Platinum — and click **Select** on the plan that matches your goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your investment amount">
    Type in the amount you want to invest, ensuring it meets the package minimum. Your available wallet balance is shown for reference.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your investment">
    Review the plan details, including your projected return range and the cycle end date, then click **Confirm Investment** to activate your plan immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once confirmed, your plan appears as **Active** on your dashboard and the 7-day countdown begins.

## Monitoring Your Investment

Your SM Mining Africa dashboard gives you a real-time view of every active plan. From the dashboard you can track:

- **Active plan** — the package tier currently running and your committed capital
- **Days remaining** — a live countdown showing how many days are left in the current 7-day cycle
- **Projected returns** — your estimated profit range at cycle completion based on your plan tier and investment amount
- **Total balance** — your combined wallet balance, including funds from completed cycles ready to reinvest or withdraw

Check your dashboard at any time to stay informed on the progress of your investments.
