SM Mining Africa credits your returns at the end of each 7-day plan cycle. This page explains how returns are calculated, when they are available, and how to access your profits — whether you choose to reinvest for compounded growth or withdraw to your crypto wallet.

<Note>
  Your principal investment is returned along with your profits at the end of each cycle. You never need to subtract your original deposit when calculating your total payout.
</Note>

## How Returns Are Calculated

Your return is determined by two factors: **the package tier you select** and **the current performance of the mining pool** during your active cycle. Each package has a target return range — a lower bound and an upper bound — based on historical mining and trading signal performance. Your actual return falls within that range depending on pool output over the 7 days.

**Example — Gold Plan ($1,000 investment):**

| Detail               | Value                   |
|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Package              | Gold                    |
| Minimum Investment   | $2,000 (example: $1,000)|
| Target Return Range  | 12–18%                  |
| Estimated Profit     | $120 – $180             |
| Total Payout at End  | $1,120 – $1,180         |
| Cycle Duration       | 7 days                  |

If you invest **$1,000** in the Gold plan (which targets 12–18%), your profit range at the end of the 7-day cycle is **$120 to $180**, giving you a total payout of **$1,120 to $1,180**.

**Example — Silver Plan ($500 investment):**

| Detail               | Value                   |
|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Package              | Silver                  |
| Investment Amount    | $500                    |
| Target Return Range  | 8–12%                   |
| Estimated Profit     | $40 – $60               |
| Total Payout at End  | $540 – $560             |
| Cycle Duration       | 7 days                  |

A **$500 Silver plan** targets 8–12% returns, producing an estimated profit of **$40 to $60** and a total payout of **$540 to $560** after 7 days.

## When Are Returns Credited?

Returns are credited to your SM Mining Africa wallet balance automatically at the **end of your 7-day cycle** — on the same day and approximately the same time your plan was activated. You do not need to take any action to receive your payout.

When your cycle completes and returns are credited, you will receive:

- **An email notification** confirming the amount credited and your updated wallet balance
- **An SMS notification** sent to the mobile number registered on your account

Log in to your dashboard at any time after the notification to see your updated balance and decide your next step.

## Reinvesting vs. Withdrawing

After each cycle completes, you have two options for your funds:

**Reinvest**

Activate a new plan immediately using some or all of your payout. Reinvesting is the most powerful way to grow your capital on the platform because your returns compound — each cycle you earn returns on a larger base than the last. Even reinvesting a portion of your profits each cycle accelerates your long-term growth significantly.

**Withdraw**

Transfer your available balance — or any portion of it — to your external crypto wallet. Withdrawals are processed to the wallet address registered on your account. See the [Withdrawals](/account/withdrawals) guide for processing times, supported assets, and minimum withdrawal amounts.

<Tip>
  Reinvesting compounds your returns. Consider reinvesting at least a portion of your profits each cycle to accelerate the growth of your portfolio over time.
</Tip>

## Profit Dashboard

Your SM Mining Africa dashboard gives you a complete picture of your earnings at a glance. From the **Profits** section you can view:

| Dashboard Metric   | What It Shows                                                              |
|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Total Invested** | The cumulative amount you have committed across all active and past plans  |
| **Total Earned**   | Your cumulative profits credited across all completed cycles               |
| **Active Plans**   | Every currently running plan, including tier, amount, and days remaining   |
| **Available Balance** | Funds in your wallet ready to reinvest or withdraw right now            |

Use the dashboard after every cycle to review your performance, compare returns across tiers, and plan your next investment.
